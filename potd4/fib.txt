I have a board of size 1 by N. I also have an infinite supply of candy corn and I am allowed to place 
candy corn in any spot on the board so long as no 2 candy corn are adjacent to each other. How many 
possible ways are there to put candy corn on the board? Include the case where the board is empty as 
well. Please print your answer modulo 2017.

Input format:
A single integer N

Output format:
The number of possible outcomes

Sample Input:
2

Sample Output:
3

Constraints:
Beginner: 1 <= N <= 15
Advanced: 1 <= N <= 10,000
